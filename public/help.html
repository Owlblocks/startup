    My Account

    This page contains the button to log out. Once logged out, you will not be able to access the rest of the website until you log in.

    Friends

    It might be hard to tell, but you can actually click to access the "Friends" page at the top of your pinned friends list.
    This is where you will be able to search for people to "friend" (it's basically just a "follow" feature) and view their user pages. You can also click the pin button next to your friends' names to pin them to your sidebar.
    You can try searching and adding 'owlblocks'

    My GIFS

    Click here to access your user page. Any gifs you upload will appear here.

    UPLOAD

    This is where you can upload gifs (with a max file size of 2 Mb) to your user page.

    FAVORITES

    Whenever you click the star in the top-left corner of a gif, it will be favorited (or unfavorited). Favorited gifs, from anyone's page, will appear on your favorites page.
